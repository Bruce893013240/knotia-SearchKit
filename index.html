Features

Built for MMPA students and practitioners

Stop hunting through dense standard text. Knotia SearchKit finds the exact paragraph that answers your question — in seconds.

🔍
Two-stage retrieval
BM25 keyword search narrows the corpus, then a cross-encoder reranker surfaces the most semantically relevant paragraphs.
🤖
HyDE expansion
Before searching, the AI generates a hypothetical answer paragraph in standard accounting language — dramatically improving BM25 recall on technical queries.
📄
Paragraph-level citations
Every answer point links directly to the source paragraph in Knotia — click to scroll and highlight the exact text in the standard.
🔒
No API key needed
Sign in with Google and top up credits. All AI calls are handled server-side — your data never touches a third-party directly from the browser.

How it works

From question to answer in seconds

01
Ask your question
Press ⌘ Shift F on any Knotia page to open the search bar. Type your question in plain English.
02
Auto-navigate + index
The AI identifies the relevant standard, navigates the Knotia TOC automatically, and builds a live BM25 index from the page DOM.
03
HyDE + reranking
A hypothetical answer expands your query, BM25 retrieves candidates, then Jina Reranker re-scores by semantic relevance.
04
Cited answer
A plain-English answer appears with paragraph-level citations. Click any point to scroll and highlight the exact source text.

How to install

  1. Click Download Extension above — save the .crx file anywhere on your computer.
  2. Open Chrome and go to chrome://extensions
  3. Enable Developer mode (toggle in the top-right corner).
  4. Drag and drop the .crx file onto the extensions page.
  5. Click Add extension in the confirmation dialog.

About

Why Knotia SearchKit exists

Knotia is the authoritative platform for Canadian accounting standards — but its built-in search is frustratingly limited. Ctrl+F only gets you so far when you're looking for a specific paragraph across hundreds of pages of dense standard text.

Knotia SearchKit is an independent third-party extension built by Bruce's Toolbox to bring modern AI retrieval — BM25, HyDE expansion, and semantic reranking — directly into the Knotia interface. It is not affiliated with Knotia or CPA Canada in any way. The tool was developed for internal use within the MMPA program.

Knotia Search works with all six major libraries available on Knotia: IFRS (IFRS 1–17), IAS (IAS 1–41), ASPE (Sections 1000–3870), CAS / CSAE / CSRE (Canadian Auditing Standards), CSQM (CSQM 1 and 2), and PS (Public Sector Accounting Standards). The extension reads live from the Knotia page you have open — so it always reflects the latest edition available in your Knotia subscription.
